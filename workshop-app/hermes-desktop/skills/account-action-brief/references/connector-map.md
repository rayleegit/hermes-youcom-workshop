# Connector Map — /account-action-brief

## Read-only (fetch only)
- You.com Search, Contents, Research, Finance Research (optional)
- CRM read, internal docs, meeting notes (sanitized)

## Draft-only (human sends)
- Email draft, Slack/Teams review request, CRM update draft

- Unapproved legal/compliance language
- Auto-send outreach, auto-CRM-update (pilot phase)

Read public web + approved internal context. Draft reviews. No auto-send.
