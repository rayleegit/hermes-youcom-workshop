# Delivery options — meeting-prep

## hermes_thread (default)

Brief appears in Hermes when you open the app before the call.

Best for: pilots, low connector overhead, workshop graduates.

## slack_dm

Hermes copies formatted Draft to a Slack DM to the workflow owner.

Best for: reps who live in Slack between meetings.

**Governance:** DM to self/owner only — not customer channels.

## email_self

Send Draft to owner's work email with subject:
`Meeting prep — [COMPANY] — [DATETIME]`

Best for: exec workflows, mobile review before calls.

## doc_draft

Append to a Notion page or Google Doc in an internal "meeting prep" folder.

Best for: teams that centralize account context.

## Multi-meeting digest

When `lookahead_hours` covers multiple meetings, deliver:

1. Digest header (count + date range)
2. One brief block per meeting (separator between each)
3. Single review pass recommended before any external use

Never batch-deliver to a shared customer-facing channel without facilitator approval.
