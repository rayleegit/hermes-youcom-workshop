# Post-Event Learning — Go Deeper After the 60-Minute Workshop

The live session is a **complete story** in 60 minutes: evidence → governance → agent start → community.

Everything below is **designed for after the event** — async, self-paced, or in your community office hours.

---

## Start here (first 48 hours)

1. **Download All Artifacts** from the workshop app wrap-up step
2. **Finish your agent** — `AGENT-BUILD-GUIDE.md` (steps 5–6 you skipped in the room)
3. **Join the You.com Discord** — https://discord.gg/2C4WgryxSD; introduce yourself (joined from this workshop)
4. **Run one test brief** — verify six sections + Draft status
