## Series

Title:
Build Governed AI Workflows with You.com

Description:

Most AI demos stop at an answer. Real teams need workflows: live web context, approved internal knowledge, CRM or support context, human review, and clear rules for when an agent can act.

This public workshop series shows how to build those workflows with You.com as the real-time web intelligence layer, Hermes as the reusable workflow layer, and NemoClaw as the trust and governance layer.

You will see how a workflow can search the live web, inspect sources, combine internal context, draft approved next steps, route risky claims for review, and prepare a pilot your team can actually run.

Attendees leave with workflow templates, connector maps, governance checklists, and a two-week pilot plan.

## Workshop 2

Title:
Build a Trusted Answer Product with You.com, Internal Docs, Support Data, Analytics, and NemoClaw

Date and time:
[DATE TBD], [TIME TBD] PT

Short description:
Design a source-backed answer experience that combines live web intelligence, internal knowledge, support signals, product context, citations, and risk-aware escalation.

Luma description:

Fast answers are easy to demo. Trusted answers are harder to ship.

This public workshop shows the product pattern behind a trusted answer experience: how a user question becomes a concise answer with sources, freshness signals, uncertainty language, follow-up questions, and a clear path to deeper research or human review.

We will position You.com's upcoming Answers API alongside Search, Contents, Research, and MCP, then show how answer products can integrate internal docs, support data, analytics signals, project trackers, and NemoClaw governance.

The goal is to help builders move from answer demo to production-ready experience.

What you will learn:
- How to distinguish raw search results, fast answers, and deeper research.
- How to design citations, source display, freshness, uncertainty, and follow-up questions.
- How internal docs, support tickets, analytics, and project trackers can improve answer quality.
- How NemoClaw can classify risk and decide when to answer, soften, escalate, or block final guidance.
- How to evaluate answer quality before launch.

Who should attend:
- Product managers and designers building answer, search, support, research, or assistant experiences
- Developers and solutions engineers evaluating You.com APIs
- Support, success, and developer-relations teams
- Trust, safety, legal, compliance, and AI governance partners

Takeaway:
You will leave with a Trusted Answer Product Kit: UX pattern, citation model, risk-routing rules, quality scorecard, and launch feedback checklist.
